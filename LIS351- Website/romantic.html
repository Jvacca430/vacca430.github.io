Romantic Movies

Five Feet Apart

Five Feet Apart is a movie about a teenage girl who has cystic fibrosis and is isolated in the confines of her home. However, through the Internet, she meets a teenage boy who is battling the same illness. Their connection, and wellbeings, are put to the test in this romantic film. Image from IMDb

The Notebook

The Notebook is one of my favorite romance movies. The plot involves two characters who fall in love with each other. The male protagonist, however, goes away to serve in the war. While viewers think that this symbolizes the end of their relationship, there is so much more to come as the movie transpires. Image from Roger Ebert

After

After is a movie about a sheltered teenage girl who has a high school boyfriend to which she is very loyal. However, once she leaves home and goes off to college, she meets a rebellious boy who challenges her to try new things. Image from Simon & Schuster
