Funny Movies

Bridesmaids

Bridesmaids is a movie about a single woman who is having struggles within her own life, yet is asked to be the maid of honor in her best friend's wedding. The single woman has barely any funds, yet sets out to plan an unforgettable wedding for her best friend. Image from IMDb Image from New York Times

The Hangover

The Hangover is a classic comedy film which follows four men who go to Las Vegas for a bachelor's party, and wake up in the morning with little memory of the events that transpired. In this movie, the men are challenged to clean up their messes and make it to the wedding on time. Image from Rotten Tomatoes

Pitch Perfect

Pitch Perfect is a comedy movie about a college student who arrives on campus and is struggling to find her true friends in a new place. She decides to try out for the a capella group in hopes of utilizing her talents and finding friends. Joining the group is just the start of her college life.
