The Last Song

The Last Song is a movie starring Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus which follows a teenage girl, Ronnie, as she stays at her father's house for the summer. She later meets Will, played by Hemsworth, and they fall in love. Ronnie is with her father and brother for the summer, and things take a turn as she is finally settling into her summer home. Image from Wikipedia

The Fault in Our Stars

The Fault in Our Stars is a movie that came out in 2014, starring Shailene Woodley as Hazel Grace Lancaster and Ansel Elgort as Gus Waters. Hazel is a teenager girl battling cancer, and she meets Gus through her support group. This coming-of-age romance film is a must-watch. Image from IMDb

A Star Is Born

A Star Is Born is a film that came out in 2018, with main characters Bradley Cooper and Lady Gaga. This movie is about a musician Jackson Maine who falls in love with Lady Gaga's character. Maine is a widely renouned artist and he helps Ally (played by Lady Gaga) to find her place under the spotlight. However, as the movie progresses, we learn more about Maine's history and the internal conflicts with which he is grappling. Image from IMDb
